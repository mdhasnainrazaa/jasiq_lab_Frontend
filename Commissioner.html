Administrative Vision & Mission

Vision

To establish Singrauli as a model municipal corporation with efficient governance, sustainable development, and citizen-centric services, befitting its status as India's Energy Capital.

Mission

  • Ensure transparent and accountable administration
  • Deliver quality civic services efficiently
  • Promote sustainable urban development
  • Foster citizen participation in governance
  • Maintain fiscal discipline and resource optimization
