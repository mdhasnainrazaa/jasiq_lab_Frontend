User Guidelines

Your comprehensive guide to using our municipal services effectively and safely

Purpose of the Website

This website provides essential services, information, and updates from the Municipal Corporation for the benefit of citizens, businesses, and visitors. Our goal is to make municipal services accessible, transparent, and efficient for all users.

How to Use Website Features

  • Use the navigation menu to explore services, departments, and announcements
  • Use the search bar to find specific content quickly
  • Forms and documents are available for download under the "Downloads" section
  • Submit forms online using the provided instructions and guidelines
  • Check the status of your applications through the tracking system

Accessibility and Language Support

This website is designed to be accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. We support screen readers and provide keyboard navigation options. Language selection options are available in both Hindi and English to serve our diverse community better.

Contact and Help Desk

Need assistance? Our support team is here to help you navigate our services and resolve any technical issues you may encounter.

Email: support@municipal.gov.in

Phone: +91 12345 67890

Hours: Monday - Friday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM

Dos and Don'ts

Do's
  • Check official announcements regularly
  • Use accurate details while filling out forms
  • Keep your login credentials secure
  • Report technical issues promptly
Don'ts
  • Share your personal information unnecessarily
  • Misuse any service provided on the site
  • Submit false or misleading information
  • Attempt to access unauthorized areas

Legal Disclaimers

The Municipal Corporation is not responsible for any misuse of content or services. All materials are subject to copyright and usage policies. Users are responsible for ensuring the accuracy of information they provide.

Please read our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use for detailed information about your rights and responsibilities.

Safe Browsing Practices

  • Use strong, unique passwords for your account access
  • Ensure your internet connection is secure before accessing sensitive services
  • Log out completely after using municipal services
  • Do not click on suspicious links claiming to be from this site
  • Verify the website URL before entering personal information
  • Keep your browser and security software updated
