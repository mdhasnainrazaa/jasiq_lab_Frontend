The Hoarding Section manages regulation, permission, and placement of advertisement hoardings, banners, and posters in the city, including election-related displays.

Permissions are issued by the Municipal Corporation based on application review and election commission guidelines.

Yes. During election periods, only approved hoardings as per the Model Code of Conduct are allowed.

You can view or download the approved list from the PDF section above or the Downloads section.

Agencies must apply through official channels, submit location details, and await inspection and approval.
