Privacy Policy

At Singrauli Municipal Corporation, we are committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of our citizens. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our digital services.

Last Updated: January 2025

1 Purpose

This website is designed to provide citizens with official information and services related to Singrauli Municipal Corporation. We respect your right to privacy and are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data protection in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

2 Information We Collect

We collect information in the following categories:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, postal address, and other identifying information when voluntarily provided through forms or service applications.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, date and time of visit, and pages accessed.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our website, including navigation patterns and feature usage.
  • Communication Records: Correspondence through contact forms, emails, or phone calls for service delivery.

3 Use of Collected Information

Your information is used solely for legitimate municipal purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Processing applications, permits, and municipal service requests
  • Communication: Responding to queries, complaints, and providing service updates
  • Website Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance user experience and functionality
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting statutory requirements and maintaining public records as mandated by law
  • Security: Protecting against fraud, unauthorized access, and maintaining system integrity

4 Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality and security
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website to improve services
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and choices for a personalized experience

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

5 Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Information may be shared only in these limited circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When mandated by courts, government agencies, or applicable laws
  • Service Delivery: With authorized government departments for specific service provision, with your consent
  • Public Safety: When necessary to protect public safety or prevent illegal activities
  • Data Processing: With trusted service providers under strict confidentiality agreements

6 Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Technical Safeguards: Encryption, secure servers, and access controls
  • Administrative Controls: Staff training, access limitations, and regular security audits
  • Physical Security: Secure facilities and controlled access to data centers
  • Regular Updates: Continuous monitoring and updating of security protocols

7 External Links

Our website may contain links to other government websites or trusted third-party services. This Privacy Policy applies only to our website. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit, as we are not responsible for their privacy practices or content.

8 Your Rights

As a citizen and user of our services, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Know: What personal data we collect and how it's used
Right to Correct: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
Right to Delete: Request deletion of your data (where legally permissible)
Right to Access: Obtain copies of your personal data we hold
Right to Object: Object to certain types of data processing
Right to Restrict: Request limitation of data processing in certain circumstances

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. All changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Modified" date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information.

For significant changes, we will provide prominent notice on our website or through direct communication where appropriate.
